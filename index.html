Exchange student in US

Exchange student of University of Colorado Boulder

After classes, there are many events for sutdents to take part in. You can meet the international students from all over the world. You can learn and experience the culture from them. During the year of exchange life in CUBoulder, I took a lot of classes such as User-Centered Design, Mobile App Development, Analysis of algorithm, etc. I met a lot of Japanese students there. Making friends with them not only experinced Japanese culture but also learned some Japanese. There are many interesting things to say about the exchange life. Food Inspector

An app for users to inspect products while shopping

There are many people who avoid specific food ingredients. The reasons are also diverse from dietary restrictions to cultural/social reasons. No matter what the reasons are about, they need to read all ingredients to figure out whether or not a product include certain ingredients that they want to avoid. For those, grocery shopping can be stressful and quite time-consuming. We aim to resolve this common but unsolved problems via our project, Food Inspector.

Want to see more ? welcome to our food Inspector blog - Food Inspector

Shopping Helper

Grocery shopping becomes easier !!

Most of the students cook in their dormitories to save some money. They buy some groceries from super markets once a week. Since they have a lot of activities, some of the groceries they bought may expire. In this project, we propose an app records all the food in fridge and show the users estimated expiration date that some groceries are going to expire. Also, we have some other features such as grocery stores websites, shopping lists and speaking calculator. Shopping Helper makes shopping easier than before.
